About SafeStamp™

SafeStamp™ is advanced nanotechnology that counterfeiters cannot replicate, allowing consumers to themselves ensure that their product came not from a criminal but from a legitimate manufacturer.

Why SafeStamp™?

Around the globe, the market value of counterfeit goods sold is estimated at $1.4 trillion. Fake medicine causes sickness and death worldwide. Combining technology complex enough to resist replication by counterfeiters with a user-interface simple enough to engage consumers and empower them to force retailers to purchase authentic product, SafeStamp™ is a completely new approach to fighting counterfeiting, unlike anything produced by security firms to date.



TECHNOLOGY

Complex proprietary nanotechnology resists replication by counterfeiters.



DESIGN

Simple, elegant and fun UI, consisting of an engaging color-reward association, empowers consumers to verify for themselves the authenticity of their products.



BRANDING

Not only allows firms to capture back market share stolen by counterfeiters, but to differentiate their brands against competitors that lack verified authenticity.
