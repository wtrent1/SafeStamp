About SafeStamp®

SafeStamp® seals medicine packaging with its nanotech authentication mark. The seal is built using advanced nanotechnology that counterfeiters cannot replicate, allowing consumers to themselves ensure that their product came not from a criminal but from a legitimate manufacturer. Our technology allows brand owners to engage with consumers to earn and grow brand loyalty while protecting the brand from supply chain fraud, including counterfeiting and theft.

Why SafeStamp®?

Around the globe, the market value of counterfeit goods sold is estimated at $653.77 billion USD. This is especially problematic in regard to medicine, as fakes cause sickness and death worldwide. The pharmaceutical industry is under increasing pressure from regulatory agencies to ensure that adulterated product is not injected into the market. The serialization approach requires a comprehensive system to track and trace the passage of medicine throughout the entire supply chain, but does not prevent retailers from never ordering the authentic drug. Combining technology complex enough to resist replication by counterfeiters with a user-interface simple enough to engage consumers and empower them to force retailers to purchase authentic product, SafeStamp™ is a completely new approach to fighting counterfeiting, unlike anything produced by security firms to date.
